Price Op

  • In the UK there is very little regulation on the premiums we can charge
  • This allows us to take a different approach to setting our premiums which doesn't just rely on the risk
  • The approach we take is to look at the customer's buying behaviour, specifically their price elasticity
  • We implement a random price trial assigning people high and low prices
  • Using this data we can estimate customer $demand$ (the probability that someone will buy)
  • $demand$ is estimated using logistic regression so will take the form
    • $$\frac{y}{1-y} = \beta_0 + \beta_1x_1 +...+\beta_nx_n$$
  • In insurance, this will use the usual rating factors along with the most important variable, the premium
    • $$Demand = \beta_0 + \beta_1age + \beta_2mileage + \beta_3premium +...+\beta_nx_n$$
  • We can look at this demand curve for an individual
